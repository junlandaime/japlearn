Introduction to Kanji Quiz

The JLPT N5 level requires knowledge of approximately 100 basic kanji characters. This quiz will help you test and improve your kanji recognition skills through multiple choice questions and matching exercises.

Each question will present you with a kanji character, and you'll need to select the correct meaning or reading. After completing each question, you'll see an explanation and additional information about the kanji.

Study Tip: When learning kanji, focus on recognizing the basic components (radicals) and practice writing the characters to help with memorization. Learning kanji in context through vocabulary and example sentences is also highly effective.

How to Use This Quiz:

  1. Choose the quiz mode (Meaning Quiz or Reading Quiz)
  2. Read the kanji and select the correct answer
  3. Check your answer and review the explanation
  4. Progress through the quiz to test your knowledge of all N5 kanji
  5. Retake the quiz regularly to reinforce your learning
