Weather Vocabulary

Learn essential Japanese words for talking about weather, climate, and seasons.

Introduction to Weather Vocabulary

Weather is a common topic of conversation in Japan, just like in many other cultures. Being able to discuss the weather is an essential part of daily communication. In this lesson, we'll explore vocabulary related to weather conditions, seasons, climate, and natural phenomena.

Key Phrase:

今日の天気はどうですか。

Kyō no tenki wa dō desu ka.

How is the weather today?

Cultural Note: Weather forecasts are a significant part of daily news in Japan. This is because Japan experiences a wide range of weather conditions throughout the year, from typhoons to heavy snowfall, and the Japanese are often very attentive to changes in weather patterns. Many Japanese people check the weather forecast regularly and plan activities accordingly.

Weather Conditions

Here are common weather conditions and terms you'll need to describe the weather in Japanese.

Japanese Romaji English Example Usage Audio
天気 (てんき) Tenki Weather 今日の天気はいいです。
(Today's weather is nice.)
晴れ (はれ) Hare Sunny/Clear 明日は晴れです。
(Tomorrow will be sunny.)
曇り (くもり) Kumori Cloudy 今日は曇りです。
(Today is cloudy.)
雨 (あめ) Ame Rain 雨が降っています。
(It's raining.)
小雨 (こさめ) Kosame Light rain/Drizzle 小雨が降っています。
(It's drizzling.)
大雨 (おおあめ) Ōame Heavy rain 大雨が降っています。
(It's pouring/raining heavily.)
雪 (ゆき) Yuki Snow 雪が降っています。
(It's snowing.)
風 (かぜ) Kaze Wind 強い風が吹いています。
(A strong wind is blowing.)
霧 (きり) Kiri Fog 今朝は霧がありました。
(There was fog this morning.)
雷 (かみなり) Kaminari Thunder 雷がなっています。
(It's thundering.)

Example Dialogue:

A: 今日の天気はどうですか。

A: Kyō no tenki wa dō desu ka?

A: How is the weather today?

B: 今日は曇りですが、午後から雨が降るでしょう。

B: Kyō wa kumori desu ga, gogo kara ame ga furu deshō.

B: Today is cloudy, but it will probably rain in the afternoon.

Temperature and Climate

These words will help you describe temperature and climate conditions in Japanese.

Japanese Romaji English Example Usage Audio
気温 (きおん) Kion Temperature 今日の気温は何度ですか。
(What is today's temperature?)
暑い (あつい) Atsui Hot (weather) 今日はとても暑いです。
(It's very hot today.)
熱い (あつい) Atsui Hot (objects) このコーヒーは熱いです。
(This coffee is hot.)
寒い (さむい) Samui Cold (weather) 冬は寒いです。
(Winter is cold.)
冷たい (つめたい) Tsumetai Cold (objects) この水は冷たいです。
(This water is cold.)
涼しい (すずしい) Suzushii Cool/Refreshing 秋は涼しいです。
(Autumn is cool.)
温かい (あたたかい) Atatakai Warm 春は温かいです。
(Spring is warm.)
湿気 (しっけ) Shikke Humidity 日本の夏は湿気が多いです。
(Japanese summers are very humid.)
乾燥 (かんそう) Kansō Dry 冬は乾燥しています。
(Winter is dry.)
度 (ど) Do Degree (temperature) 今日は25度です。
(It's 25 degrees today.)

Language Note: Japanese has different words for "hot" and "cold" depending on whether you're describing weather or objects. Use 暑い (atsui) for hot weather and 熱い (atsui) for hot objects. Similarly, use 寒い (samui) for cold weather and 冷たい (tsumetai) for cold objects.

Natural Weather Phenomena

Japan experiences various natural weather phenomena throughout the year. Here are some important terms related to these events.

Japanese Romaji English Example Usage Audio
台風 (たいふう) Taifū Typhoon 台風が来ています。
(A typhoon is coming.)
地震 (じしん) Jishin Earthquake 昨日地震がありました。
(There was an earthquake yesterday.)
津波 (つなみ) Tsunami Tsunami 津波の警報が出ています。
(A tsunami warning has been issued.)
洪水 (こうずい) Kōzui Flood 大雨で洪水になりました。
(The heavy rain caused a flood.)
虹 (にじ) Niji Rainbow 雨の後に虹が出ました。
(A rainbow appeared after the rain.)
雷雨 (らいう) Raiu Thunderstorm 昨夜は雷雨でした。
(There was a thunderstorm last night.)
嵐 (あらし) Arashi Storm 嵐の中で運転しました。
(I drove in a storm.)
竜巻 (たつまき) Tatsumaki Tornado 竜巻が家を破壊しました。
(The tornado destroyed houses.)
稲妻 (いなずま) Inazuma Lightning 稲妻が空を照らしました。
(Lightning lit up the sky.)
吹雪 (ふぶき) Fubuki Blizzard 吹雪で電車が止まりました。
(The trains stopped because of the blizzard.)

Example Dialogue:

A: 明日台風が来るそうです。

A: Ashita taifū ga kuru sō desu.

A: I heard a typhoon is coming tomorrow.

B: そうですか。傘を持っていきます。

B: Sō desu ka. Kasa o motte ikimasu.

B: Is that so? I'll bring an umbrella.

Seasons and Seasonal Events

Japan has four distinct seasons, each with its own characteristics and seasonal events. Here are the vocabulary words for seasons and related terms.

Japanese Romaji English Example Usage Audio
季節 (きせつ) Kisetsu Season 日本には四つの季節があります。
(There are four seasons in Japan.)
春 (はる) Haru Spring 春は花が咲きます。
(Flowers bloom in spring.)
夏 (なつ) Natsu Summer 夏は暑いです。
(Summer is hot.)
秋 (あき) Aki Autumn/Fall 秋は葉が赤くなります。
(Leaves turn red in autumn.)
冬 (ふゆ) Fuyu Winter 冬は雪が降ります。
(It snows in winter.)
梅雨 (つゆ) Tsuyu Rainy season 梅雨は6月と7月です。
(The rainy season is in June and July.)
桜 (さくら) Sakura Cherry blossom 春に桜が咲きます。
(Cherry blossoms bloom in spring.)
紅葉 (もみじ) Momiji Autumn leaves/Maple 京都の紅葉はきれいです。
(The autumn leaves in Kyoto are beautiful.)
花見 (はなみ) Hanami Cherry blossom viewing 友達と花見に行きました。
(I went cherry blossom viewing with friends.)
暦 (こよみ) Koyomi Calendar 日本の暦には多くの祝日があります。
(There are many holidays in the Japanese calendar.)

Cultural Note: Seasons are deeply important in Japanese culture and are frequently referenced in literature, art, and daily life. Many Japanese traditions and festivals are tied to specific seasons, such as cherry blossom viewing (花見, hanami) in spring and moon viewing (月見, tsukimi) in autumn. The changing of seasons is celebrated and observed with particular attention in Japan.

Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Match the Japanese words with their English meanings

Japanese Words:

  1. 晴れ (はれ)
  2. 雨 (あめ)
  3. 雪 (ゆき)
  4. 台風 (たいふう)
  5. 寒い (さむい)

English Meanings:

  1. Snow
  2. Typhoon
  3. Cold (weather)
  4. Sunny
  5. Rain

Exercise 2: Complete the sentences

  1. 今日は＿＿＿です。空に雲がありません。(Today is ___. There are no clouds in the sky.)

    Options: 雨 (ame), 晴れ (hare), 曇り (kumori)

  2. 冬はとても＿＿＿です。(Winter is very ___.)

    Options: 暑い (atsui), 寒い (samui), 暖かい (atatakai)

  3. 傘を持って行きましょう。＿＿＿が降っています。(Let's take an umbrella. It's ___.)

    Options: 雨 (ame), 風 (kaze), 雪 (yuki)

  4. 日本の＿＿＿には桜が咲きます。(Cherry blossoms bloom in Japanese ___.)

    Options: 夏 (natsu), 冬 (fuyu), 春 (haru)

  5. 今日の＿＿＿は何度ですか。(What is today's ___ in degrees?)

    Options: 気温 (kion), 風 (kaze), 天気 (tenki)

Exercise 3: Translate the following sentences

  1. How is the weather today?

  2. It's raining heavily now.

  3. Summer in Japan is very humid.

  4. I like autumn because it's cool.

  5. Be careful, a typhoon is coming tomorrow.
