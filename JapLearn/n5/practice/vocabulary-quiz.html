Introduction to Vocabulary Quiz

The JLPT N5 level requires knowledge of approximately 800 basic vocabulary words. This quiz will help you test and improve your vocabulary recognition skills through multiple choice questions and matching exercises.

Each question will present you with a Japanese vocabulary word, and you'll need to select the correct meaning or usage. After completing each question, you'll see an explanation and additional information about the word.

Study Tip: When learning vocabulary, focus on using the words in context rather than memorizing isolated words. Create simple sentences with new vocabulary and try to use them in daily conversations or writing practice.

How to Use This Quiz:

  1. Choose a vocabulary category to focus on
  2. Read the Japanese word and select the correct meaning
  3. Check your answer and review the explanation
  4. Progress through the quiz to test your knowledge of N5 vocabulary
  5. Retake quizzes regularly to reinforce your learning
