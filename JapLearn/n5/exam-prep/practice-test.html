JLPT N5 Practice Test

This practice test is designed to simulate the actual JLPT N5 exam. It includes all the sections you'll find in the real test: Vocabulary, Grammar, Reading, and Listening. You can complete the entire test at once or practice each section separately.

Time Management: The full JLPT N5 exam takes approximately 145 minutes to complete. We recommend setting a timer to simulate real exam conditions.

Test your knowledge of basic Japanese vocabulary, kanji, and grammar structures.

  • 30 grammar questions
